Tron1A Point-Foot Biped Locomotion

Biped Point-Foot Locomotion

Project Overview

This project develops locomotion for a point-foot biped platform (Tron1A), focusing on state estimation, gait generation, and stability under disturbances.

Demonstration

  • ✓ Consistent traversals over mixed terrains with balanced CoM motion
  • ✓ Reliable transitions between gait phases without foot scuffing
  • ✓ Video evidence shows robustness to moderate disturbances
